goChem

A library for computational chemistry.

Dependencies of the core goChem library and its extensions

  • Gonum, Numerical libraries for Go
  • The xdrfile library for reading XTC trajectory files, from Gromacs.

goChem friends

  • Go, the programming language in which goChem is written.
  • PyMOL, a powerful and open-source molecular visualization program.

goChem-compatible QM programs

  • xtb.
  • Turbomole.
  • Mopac2012.
  • Orca.
  • NWChem.

Other Resources

  • Go Tour, a quick way to learn Go.
  • The Go documentation.
  • Go-nuts, the official Go mailing list.
  • Biogo, a bioinformatics library for the Go language.

Places where the goChem team works/has worked

  • The The Faculty of Sciences, at Universidad de Tarapaca
  • The The Chemistry and Biology Faculty, at Universidad de Santiago de Chile
  • The Christian Ochsenfeld group, at Ludwig-Maximilian University of Munich (LMU).
  • The Laboratory for instruction in Swedish at the department of Chemistry, University of Helsinki, where goChem development started.
  • The Theoretical Inorganic Chemistry lab at the Faculty of Sciences, Universidad de Chile.
